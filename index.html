My mission is to help readers with data literacy

Exposure and practice is the key to learning

Data literacy is very important these days, especially with the rapidly changing priorities in today's environment.

In the past, I used to ask people interpret data for me. However, that is a short term solution to a long term problem.

Literacy is important, period. Teaching a man to fish is the best way to empower people to make the best decisions throughout their life.

The new generation must be able to interpret data in inform actionable decision making.

The goal of this blog is to help teach readers how to interpret data, one simple step at a time.

Let's start with a simple and relevant example.

I will use the Walkscore API to help users understand how to read walkability and transit scores in the area that they live in.

Very excited to start this journey with all of you. Let's begin!

Special thanks to all platforms that make data accessible for educational purposes.
